Galaxy Music Festival

  • Subjects

    Branding
    Visual Identity
    Event Deisgn

A promotional campaign for a music event that will be aired on MTV television. My role is to pick a topic and do a thorough research, ideation and conceptualisation of topic into a deliverable.

I got inspired by the diversity of the indie muscians and named this music festival Galaxy as each artist is just like a planet in the galaxy.
I came up with a design language and create application collaterals according to concepts of the indie communities I appreacited, self-expression are powerful, and idea is stronger than boundaries.

I used a 3D planet logo to represent the concept of galaxy, and gave the planet a cheese ball texture to show the uniqueness.

The chosen solution is based on high contrast colours, halftone, maximalism or anti-minimalism, the brightness of which is deliberately faded, and accented typographic elements, chaotically scattered throughout the format.
